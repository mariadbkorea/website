Unlock AI Power: Vector Search Now Native in MariaDB

Are you leveraging AI models, LLMs, or embedding-based search, and looking to integrate AI capabilities directly into your MariaDB database?

With the January launch of MariaDB Enterprise Platform 2025, MariaDB now natively supports vector embedded search. This allows you to handle both vector and OLTP workloads within a single MariaDB database, eliminating the complexity of managing separate vector databases.

To see how to power GenAI applications using your existing operational database, join our live session led by developer advocate Alejandro Duarte:

Join live on March 26: Introduction to AI-Powered Applications: LLMs, Vector Search & MariaDB
