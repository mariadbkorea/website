Introducing the MariaDB Enterprise Kubernetes Operator

Are you spending more time wrestling with database deployments and management than focusing on innovation? There’s a new way to simplify your database deployment and operation on Kubernetes.

Read on to learn about the MariaDB Enterprise Kubernetes Operator – like having an expert DBA embedded directly into your Kubernetes environment, automating the entire life cycle of your MariaDB databases.This powerful tool simplifies deployment and operation, enhances security and compliance, ensures high availability, future-proofs your infrastructure, and provides peace of mind with a Red Hat OpenShift certified solution, guaranteeing enterprise-grade support.
